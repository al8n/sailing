# slow_follower_after_compaction (etcd raft#31 regression): with a LIMITED in-flight capacity, a
# follower that briefly drops out during a burst of proposals falls behind; the leader snapshots and
# compacts its log past the follower's position, so when the follower returns it can only be caught up
# via InstallSnapshot (the compacted entries are gone) — not plain AppendEntries.
#
