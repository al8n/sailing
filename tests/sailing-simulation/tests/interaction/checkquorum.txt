# CheckQuorum: an isolated leader must step down. Node 1 leads {1,2,3} with check-quorum on; we
# isolate it from the followers, then advance two election-timeout windows: the first resets the
# activity flags (it heard from 2,3 during the initial replication), the second — now isolated — finds
# no quorum active and steps the leader down to follower. The majority {2,3} then elect a new leader.
add-nodes 3 voters=(1,2,3) checkquorum=true
